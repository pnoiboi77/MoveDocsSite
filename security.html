Security

Security is our Chief Concern!

MoveDocs secured data center, facilities and operations are Safe Harbor certified.
MoveDocs also encrypts your data at transfer with high-grade SSL and at rest with 256-bit AES.

FEDERAL AND STATE REGULATION COMPLIANCE

All MoveDocs.com Health processes and procedures have been reviewed by counsel and found to be in compliance with current Federal and State regulations, including the HIPAA Security Rule (proposed) and the HIPAA Privacy Rule. MoveDocs.com takes rigorous, precise, and accurate steps to ensure privacy for its customers, their clients, and record providers.

HIPAA COMPLIANCE

MoveDocs service, security, and privacy are vital to business success. Utilizing MoveDocs state-of-the-art technology and encrypted online document transmission techniques ensure that every document, retrieved is fully secure. The industry standard 256-bit SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technique is employed for all transmissions.

SAFEGUARDING PRIVACY

MoveDocs retrieves sensitive information for and on behalf of customers. All information collected is made available to authorized customers and their employees, and to authorized MoveDocs employees on an as-needed basis. No personally identifiable protected health information is used for sales, marketing, or non-business related activities.

ELECTRONIC DATA TRANSFER

Transmitting data electronically through the cloud is one of the fastest means for relaying medical information, however, the most pressing concern the industry faces is absolute security of that information.
MoveDocs utilizes the latest technology to ensure that applications, computers and computer networks are secure, so patient information remains confidential. Specifically, secure access to computer hardware, advanced firewall protection; secure operation systems, and the use of advanced encryption.
